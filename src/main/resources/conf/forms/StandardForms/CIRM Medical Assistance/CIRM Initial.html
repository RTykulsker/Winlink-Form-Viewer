Keep the medicine chest up to date. Ensure compliance with your Flag State. If possible do not administer any medicines before consulting C.I.R.M or qualified doctor.

The international Radio Medical Center (C.I.R.M) is the Italian Telemedical Maritime Assistance Service (TMAS). Our Mission is to provide round the clock free telemedical assistance to patients onboard ships flying any flag of any nationality all over the world. We suggest contacting C.I.R.M promptly in all cases of ill or injured persons, possibly before any treatment. This to avoid complication of pathologies or modifications in their course by inappropriate treatment. A Quick way to get in touch with us is to fill out the form and email it to us at telesoccorso@cirm.it. Alternatively you can call us at +39 06 59290263.
